Welcome to *Tyler N's Snippets of Experiences, Knowledge, and Code*, a publication that includes:

- Short and long articles about specific experiences and what I learned from them
- Short and long articles containing my knowledge and insights about specific categories
- Short and medium articles containing specific code snippets, what they are, and why they are important
- Short and long articles about my viewpoints on specific topics

> This publication is for people interested and unintrested in technology


### When I make articles:

There is no specific timeframe for when I make articles. 
Any patterns in my writing schedule are not due to established timeframes, but rather, due to
internal or external, limiting or enabling factors.
